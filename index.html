Home

Welcome to the 2025 Robotics Hackathon at the University of Manchester! This exciting event brings together 300 participants from across the country to challenge their skills in a 24-hour, adrenaline-pumping competition. Whether you're a beginner looking to dive into the world of robotics or an experienced engineer ready to tackle advanced challenges, there's something for everyone.

The event will feature three distinct challenges designed to test your creativity, problem-solving abilities, and technical expertise across both software and hardware domains. Each challenge is carefully curated to cater to all skill levels, ensuring an inclusive experience while providing a platform for participants to push their limits.
